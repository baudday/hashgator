Welcome to hashgator

We are extremely excited to present the new, and dramatically improved, hashgator! We've stepped up our game in several areas. Most notably is the improved design of the UI, but here are some other things to get excited about.

A New Way of Displaying Data

We've put a lot of hard work into processing the data that we get back from the networks we aggregate. Since we aim to expose you to interesting content from across social networks, we've taken efforts to make sure we're presenting the content in a meaningful way.

New Features Faster

We've completely redesigned our codebase for maintainability. This means you can expect us to crank out new features at a much faster pace than in the past!

New Features

We're excited to announce one of our most exciting recent feature additions, Related Tags! With related tags, you'll see a list of suggested tags that relate to what you're currently looking at. This, like many of our features, is aimed at allowing you to continue to discover new content.

Upcoming Features

Here's what we have planned for the future:

Event Pages
One of the coolest things people use hashgator for is following live events. We think this is an excellent use of the platform. So to cater to this, we will provide links directly to popular events as they happen.
Tag Collections
Being able to follow one tag across several networks is great. Being able combine several tags into a highly customized search? That's powerful. We look forward to bringing this to you in the near future.
User Accounts
Take your tags with you on any device, upvote tiles, customize your feeds so you see exactly what you're interested in. User accounts will allow you to do this and much more.
We are also always open to suggestions for new features. So feel free to tweet us @hashgator!

Missing Features

Because of the major overhaul to our codebase, some features have not yet been reimplemented. You'll notice the refresh button and the network filters are missing. You can expect the network filters to be up soon. As for the refresh button, we will be replacing that with infinite scrolling so you'll never have to stop browsing!

One big thing you'll notice missing is the Twitter Feed. Twitter has made some major changes to their API. All requests to the Twitter API have to now be authorized. Because of this, client-side requests are no longer accepted. Not to worry, we're working on getting the Twitter Feed back ASAP!
